Egg Sandwhich with Bacon!

Yearning for a yummy sandwich, which can be made in just a few minutes? Then try this protein-rich Egg sandwich recipe and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon chipotle in adobo sauce
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 slices Cheddar
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 slice bacon, halved
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Boil, peel and chop the eggs
  2. Add the seasoning and sources to the chopped eggs
  3. Spread the egg mixture on toasted bread & cover with another slice
